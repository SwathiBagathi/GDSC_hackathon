About Dogs

Try to secure the dog and ensure that it receives immediate food, water and medical treatment. Use Facebook groups, WhatsApp, or other social media platforms to circulate images of the dog. Ask around the area for lost dog posters. If you receive any claims of ownership, thoroughly verify it before giving up the dog. Try for adoption of the dog if no owner is found. If you love dogs, then you probably want to save them. Although this might seem like an overwhelming task, there are many ways you can help dogs.

About Cows

The cow is the most revered among all the animals in the Hindu tradition and has been part of the Indian culture since time immemorial. This tradition got delineated in India slowly and steadily over the decades and has reached a level that the society needs some movement to save the cow.

About Cats

Your cat is probably like a child to you, and, like any loving pet parent, you want to do everything you can to keep them healthy and safe. Cats can be very independent. They tend to take pretty good care of themselves. However, they can’t do everything on their own, so it’s up to us to be responsible. We all need to educate ourselves on the dangers cats face and how to help them if something goes wrong.
